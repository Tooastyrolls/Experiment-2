Ruel

British-Australian singer Ruel is known for the way he blends pop and R&B music and rich vocals. He became popular in his teens because of hits like "Dazed & Confused" and "Painkiller", which showcased a sound that was mature for his age.

James Arthur

James Arthur is a British singer-songwriter who became famous after winning the ninth season of The X Factor in 2012. Following the success of his first song, an arrangement of "Impossible", he went on to record a number of popular albums, such as Back from the Edge and Pisces.

ATEEZ

The South Korean boy band ATEEZ, signed to KQ Entertainment, is famous for their energetic live performances, eye-catching visuals, and varied musical style that combines pop, hip-hop, and EDM. They have received praise from all over the world for their appealing stage presence and ideas that are centered around lore since their 2018 debut.
